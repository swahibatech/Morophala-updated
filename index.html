About us

We are Morophala Holding

A 100% citizen owned company based in Botswana. A key target for us is becoming an industry leader in transport and logistics solutions. We bring you logistical convenience and comfort in the most convenient, timely, affordable and innovative manner. We aim to achieve customer satisfaction to ensure customer retention in all our work. This includes incorporating modern and advanced technologies and providing the best quality. Client retention and satisfaction cuts across all our deliverables. We specialize in Transport (local and cross border), Logistics and General Supplies.

VISION

To be a world class company of choice, a trusted and reliable leader in the transport and logistics industry

MISSION

Delivering the best quality logistics and transport solutions efficiently, conveniently and innovatively.
